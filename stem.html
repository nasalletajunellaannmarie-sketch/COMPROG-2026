Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

STEM, which stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, is one of the most popular strands under the Academic Track in Senior High School in the Philippines. It is also offered as part of LCBA’s Senior High School (SHS) programs.

STEM is perfect for students who want challenging subjects, future-ready skills, and careers in science and technology.

This strand is designed for students who are interested in:

  • Science experiments and discoveries
  • Technology and innovation
  • Engineering and design
  • Advanced mathematics and problem-solving

What You Learn in STEM Strand

STEM students take subjects that focus on research, computation, and scientific skills, such as:

  • General Biology
  • General Chemistry
  • General Physics
  • Pre-Calculus and Basic Calculus
  • Research and Scientific Investigation
  • Engineering-related concepts

Best College Courses After STEM

STEM is the best choice if you want to take courses like:

  • Engineering (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, etc.)
  • Medicine / Nursing / Pharmacy
  • Computer Science / Information Technology
  • Computer Science / Information Technology
  • Architecture
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Mathematics and Statistics

Future Careers for STEM Students

Graduates often become:

  • Engineers
  • Doctors or healthcare professionals
  • Scientists or researchers
  • Programmers and software developers
  • Architects
  • Data analysts
